About the TrustBot Study

Study Purpose

The TrustBot study is designed to explore how users interact with chatbot interfaces and how these interactions influence their perception of trustworthiness. The study aims to identify key factors that contribute to or detract from user trust in automated systems, which is critical in an age where AI-driven interfaces are increasingly common in both personal and professional settings.

Testing Scenarios

To accurately assess how the TrustBot handles different types of interactions and how users respond, we have developed a series of scenarios. These scenarios are categorized into three levels: Red, Amber, and Green. Each level presents the bot with different challenges to evaluate its performance, reliability, and trustworthiness from the user's perspective.
